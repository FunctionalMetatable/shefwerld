The Shef Kerbi News Network is a group joke art project created by members of the TBG Forums website. It is a fictional news network with satirical and otherwise humurous articles about events in a fictional universe that is similar to the Kirby universe from the Nintendo series of the same name.
The Shef Kerbi News Network was started on the TBG Forums on September 22nd, 2018. This website is simply an extension of the original roleplay and doesn't replace it in any way.
No one related to the Shef Kerbi news network is affiliated with Nintendo in any way.
